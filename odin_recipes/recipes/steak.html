Steak

Description

Steak is just steak, juicyy, flavourful and mouth-watering

Ingredients

  • rib-eye steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Canola oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat stainless-steel pan with oil in it until a bit of smoke is coming out
  2. Season the steak with the desired amount of salt and pepper
  3. Place steak in pan and let it sear for 1 minute or until a crust is formed
  4. Turn around and repeat
  5. Cook until the desired doneness is reached, either with a thermometer or by feel
  6. Let the steak rest for at least 10 minutse
  7. Enjoy!
