Pasta

Description

Simple and delicious homemade pasta, taste a lot better than store pasta

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Tomato sacue

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients
  2. Knead the dough until smooth and bouncy
  3. Stretch out as thin as possible either with a rollling pin or a pasta machine
  4. Cut into desired shape
  5. Boil water in pan
  6. Cook pasta in water for 90 seconds
  7. Combine with tomato sauce and enjoy!
