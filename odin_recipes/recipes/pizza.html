Pizza

Description

It is one of the best foods you will ever eat in your lifetime. It is a simple dish, yet one of the most delicious that will touch your tongue

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Yeast
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Other toppings

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients
  2. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth
  3. Put in a bowl, cover it with a piece of cloth
  4. Let it rise for at least 24 hours
  5. Cut into smaller portions if desired
  6. Stretch the dough in a circular shape, add the tomato sauce, cheese and rest of the toppings
  7. Pre-heat the oven to 500 celcius
  8. Put the pizza in the oven and cook for 4 minutes, rotating it along its z axis half way through
  9. Take out and enjoy!
